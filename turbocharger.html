Turbocharger Design: Boosting Performance with Precision Engineering

Key Takeaways

  • Designed a hydrogen-fueled turbocharger prototype with 68% efficiency.
  • Enhanced engineering precision using advanced simulation tools and validation techniques.

Objective

  • Develop a car turbocharger that operates efficiently using hydrogen as a fuel source.

Approach

  • Pre-scaled turbine blades using Euler’s equation and Cordier’s diagram.
  • Designed the turbine and volute using CATIA V5.
  • Conducted flow simulations in StarCCM+ to validate performance.
  • Created a 3D-printed prototype for hands-on testing and refinement.

Results

  • Achieved a prototype efficiency of 68% with hydrogen fuel.
  • Demonstrated the feasibility of a hydrogen-fueled turbocharger in simulations and tests.
