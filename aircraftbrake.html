Innovating Aircraft Brake System Design

Key Takeaways

  • Successfully designed a compact carbon/carbon brake system tailored for aircraft applications.
  • Utilized advanced Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Finit Element Analysis (FEA) tools to ensure compliance with stringent mechanical constraints.
  • Achieved lightweight design without compromising energy absorption capacity.

Objective

  • Develop a lightweight and high-performance brake system to enhance aircraft safety and efficiency while meeting design constraints.

Approach

  • Dimensioning Calculations:
    • Determined the number and size of rotor discs, stator discs, and pistons to meet performance requirements.
  • Mechanical Design:
    • Sized the screwing system and bearings to ensure durability under operational loads.
    • Created a 3D model using CATIA V5 for assembly and part design.
  • Validation:
    • Performed finite element analysis to simulate and validate the brake system under real-world conditions.

Results

  • Delivered a CAD model of a compact and efficient brake system with enhanced energy absorption capacity.
  • Validated the design using FEA, ensuring safety and compliance with operational requirements.
