Design and Optimization of a Speed Reducer

Key Takeaways

  • Developed a compact and efficient speed reducer for industrial applications.
  • Demonstrated expertise in mechanical design and stress analysis.
  • Optimized the design to enhance durability and reduce material costs.

Objective

  • To design a speed reducer capable of high torque transmission while minimizing size and weight.

Approach

  • Requirements Analysis:
    • Determined key design parameters such as input/output torque, speed ratio, and mechanical constraints.
  • Conceptual Design:
    • Utilized gear ratio calculations and material selection to ensure optimal performance.
  • CAD Modeling and Simulation:
    • Designed components using CATIA V5, ensuring accurate assembly and integration.

Results

  • Delivered a reliable and high-performance speed reducer design.
  • Validated design parameters through simulation, achieving an optimal balance of strength and weight.
  • Produced technical documentation and CAD models for manufacturing readiness.
