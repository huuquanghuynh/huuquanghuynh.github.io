About Me

Hello, I'm Quang, a passionate Mechanical Engineer with a Master's in Robotics from UC Berkeley and a strong foundation in Mechanical and Industrial Engineering from Arts et Métiers Paris. I specialize in Control Systems, Robotics, and Product Design, with expertise in programming (Python, C++, ROS2, MATLAB), machine learning (PyTorch, TensorFlow), and CAD modeling (CATIA V5, SolidWorks).

My hands-on experience includes developing advanced control algorithms like Kalman Filters and Model Predictive Controllers, designing autonomous systems in ROS2, and implementing motion planning strategies for autonomous vehicles. Highlights of my work include optimizing vehicle dynamics for AI Racing Tech's autonomous IndyCar and leading the development of a fully autonomous go-kart prototype that placed 2nd at a national competition.

I'm driven by the challenge of solving complex problems and aspire to contribute to cutting-edge projects in robotics and autonomous vehicles, where I can apply my skills to create impactful and innovative solutions.
